Welcome to my homepage! I am a Ph.D. candidate in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Boise State University. I am a research assistant in Dr.Mehrpouyan research group.
I received my B.Sc. and my M.Sc with honor in Electrical Engineering, Communication Systems from the Iran University of Science and Technology.

My research interests include machine learning, optimization, and high performance computing with their applications in communications and networking.
